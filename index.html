About Dory Abeyta

I feel embarrassed writing about myself. Interactions with them me are often brief and awkward. A lot of people think that I am rude, or disinterested, but that is usually not the case.

I like cats and my personality is often compared to them; enigmatic, aloof, and picky. However, as is often the case with cats being misunderstood, these characterizations are usually only surface level when directed at me. With patience, I can open up and become more friendly and approachable.
